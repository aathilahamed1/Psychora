# PSYCHORA – AI Powered Campus Mental Health Platform

Psychora is a privacy-first, AI-driven mental health support platform designed for colleges and universities. It enables students to access **anonymous emotional support**, complete clinically validated screenings, book counseling sessions, and engage in moderated peer communities — while institutions receive real-time anonymized insights to understand student wellbeing trends.

---

## 🚀 Key Capabilities
- 🤖 **Anonymous AI Mental Health Chatbot**
  - Early detection, mental health first aid
  - PHQ-9 / GAD-7 based screening assistance
- 🎥 **Online Counseling & Booking**
  - Verified counselors
  - Audio / Video tele-counseling
- 🗣️ **Regional-Language Psychoeducation**
- 👥 **Peer Support Community**
  - Moderated & safe
- 🛠 **Admin & Analytics Dashboard**
  - Real-time anonymized insights
  - Campus mental health trends
- 🎯 **Engagement Features**
  - Monthly surveys
  - 7-day well-being pathways
  - Mini games + daily streaks

---

## 🧠 Why Psychora?
- Reduces stigma through anonymity  
- Enables **early screening & intervention**
- Bridges counseling gaps in **rural & semi-urban** campuses  
- Localized, culturally sensitive mental health resources  
- Actionable institutional insights for policy and support decisions

## 🔄 Process Flow
1️⃣ Student accesses platform (web / mobile browser)  
2️⃣ AI chatbot interacts anonymously and supports screening  
3️⃣ System recommends support pathway  
4️⃣ Student books counseling or accesses content  
5️⃣ Admin dashboard shows anonymized analytics
